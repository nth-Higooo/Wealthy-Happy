OBJECTIVE

Our main objectives are to analyze the correlation between GDP and GNH, identify key influencing factors, assess the reliability of these measures, and determine whether GNH can predict economic success.

KEY QUESTIONS

1. Is there a correlation between a country's Gross Domestic Product (GDP) and its Gross National Happiness (GNH)?
2. What are the key factors that influence the relationship between GDP and GNH?
3. Can GDP be considered a reliable predictor of GNH, and vice versa?
4. Can GNH be used as a predictive measure for economic success and growth?
